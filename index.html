About Regnant

Founded with a passion for architectural storytelling, Regnant Design bridges the gap between technical blueprints and emotional experiences.

Led by Parth Ukani, our studio specializes in high-end CGI that helps architects sell their vision and developers sell their properties. We don't just render walls; we render lifestyles. Every shadow, reflection, and texture is meticulously crafted to create an atmosphere that feels undeniably real.
